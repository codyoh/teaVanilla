Vata Tea

Vata Dosha - one of the three Ayurvedic mind-body types - Ruled by the elements of air & ether. Vata personifies cold, dry, light, quick, irregular, & changeable characteristics. This dosha expresses joy, vitality, & clarity when in balance. When out of balance, Vata is prone to anxiety, restlessness, & nervousness.
